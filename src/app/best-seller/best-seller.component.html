    In India, it was introduced by the Chinese of Calcutta. It is usually offered Hakka-style, with gravy. Catering to vegetarian diets, there is an Indian variant, vegetable chow mein, which consists of noodles with cabbage, bamboo shoots, pea pods, green peppers, and carrots.

    Biryani

    Biryani is a mixed rice dish. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and meat usually that of chicken,mutton and sometimes, in addition, eggs or vegetables such as potatoes in certain regional varieties.
